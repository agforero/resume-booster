Resume Optimizer

by Agustin Forero (agf48@cornell.edu)

GitHub • LinkedIn • Personal Page

How to use: paste your resume and your job description of choice in the entry fields below. Then, the tool will scan your resume and attempt to find synonyms in the job description. For example, if your resume uses the word "make" where the job description uses "develop", the tool will show you the line in question alongside the verb you might want to change. A strong resume will feature verbs used in the job description, and is more likely to be selected by automatic resume readers and human recruiters alike.

Do note that this tool is not perfect: it looks for every verb in your resume, including those in headers and company names, and synonyms offered aren't always the flashiest. Even still, you may find some relevant changes to be made!
